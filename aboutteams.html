Teams we have

There are eight teams.

Mumbia Indians

Mumbai Indians (MI) is the Mumbai-based Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise that plays its home matches at Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai. It is the only team to win the IPL title five times (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019 and 2020).

Chennai Super Kings

Chennai Super Kings(CSK)is the Chennai-based Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise that plays its home matches at M.A Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai. It is the only team to win the IPL title four times (2010, 2011, 2018and 2021) .

Royal Challengers

The Royal Challengers have never won the IPL but finished runners-up on three occasions between 2009 and 2016. Their lack of success over the years despite the presence of various notable players has earned them the tag of "underachievers".

Kolkata Knight Riders

The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) are a franchise cricket team representing the city of Kolkata in the Indian Premier League. The franchise is owned by Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan, actress Juhi Chawla and her spouse Jay Mehta. The Knight Riders play at the iconic Eden Gardens stadium.It is the only team to win the IPL title two times 2012 and 2014.

Sunrises Hyderabad

The Sunrises Hyderabad(SRH) are a franchise cricket team representing the city of Hyderabad in the Indian Premier League. The franchise is owned by Kalanithi Maran of the SUN Group. It is the only team to win the IPL title one time 2016.

Rajasthan Royals

The Rajasthan Royals (RR) is the Jaipur Rajasthan-based Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise that plays its home matches at Sawai Mansingh Stadium in Jaipur.It is the only team to win the IPL title one time 2008

Delhi Capitals

The Delhi Capitals (DC) is the Delhi-based Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise that plays its home matches at Arun Jaitley Stadium in Delhi.Delhi Capitals have never won the IPL.

Punjab Kings

The Punjab Kings (PBKS) is the Punjab-based Indian Premier League (IPL) franchise that plays its home matches at PCA Stadium in Mohali.Punjab Kings have never won the IPL.
